Privacy Policy

Last updated:

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how INTOON ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your personal data when you use our app and services.

2. Data We Collect

a. Account Information

  • Email, name and user ID provided by Google or Apple authentication (we do not store passwords).

b. Usage Data

  • App activity (soundscapes created, duration, timestamps)
  • Device information (model, OS version, language, crash logs)

c. Payment Information

Subscription transactions are handled by Apple App Store (StoreKit). We do not store or process card details locally; Apple handles billing data.

d. Audio Data

We temporarily store generated soundscapes and metadata (user input text, chosen parameters). Saved audio and associated metadata are retained in your account until deleted. We do not share or store data with specific sound library providers.

3. Use of AI

Our app uses Gemini AI to generate and improve soundscapes. AI processing is transient; only necessary metadata is stored for user playback and debugging.

4. How We Use the Data

  • Authenticate users
  • Provide and personalize the service
  • Manage subscriptions and deliver paid content
  • Improve app performance

5. Data Retention

We retain user data while the account is active and remove personal data and saved audio within 30 days after account deletion.

6. Sharing of Data

We only share information when necessary: for authentication (Apple/Google), payments (Apple), or to comply with legal obligations. We never sell user data.

7. Your Rights

You may access, export, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. Contact us at cencencwy@gmail.com.

8. Data Security

We use HTTPS/TLS and industry standard practices but cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. International Transfers & Governing Law

Data may be processed outside your country but under equivalent protections. This policy is governed by the laws of the publisher jurisdiction.
