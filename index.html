What is INTOON?

INTOON is an intelligent soundscape generator powered by AI. Describe how you want to feel, and INTOON builds a deeply immersive ambience—rainy forests, calm oceans, warm cafés, distant thunder, and more.

Every soundscape is generated uniquely for you, based on your mood and intention.
