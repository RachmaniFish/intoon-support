Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long does generation take?
A: Typical short-scenes render in under 30s. Long/high-quality exports may take up to a few minutes depending on device/server load. We optimize the pipeline to reduce timeouts.
Q: Where are my generated soundscapes stored?
A: Generated audio and metadata are stored temporarily in your account. You can delete saved audio in app settings. We retain data for up to 30 days after deletion requests.
Q: How does the subscription billing work?
A: Premium is billed via Apple App Store at USD 49.99/year. Renewals and refunds are handled by Apple. See Terms for details.
Q: Who can I email for support?
A: For account, billing and content issues contact: cencencwy@gmail.com.

Troubleshooting

  • If a generation errors, try reducing duration or quality setting.
  • For frequent timeouts, use shorter scenes and fewer simultaneous layers.
  • Provide the scene description and timestamp when contacting support.

Developer / Integration

For API, blueprint format, or tone.js scheduling integration questions email cencencwy@gmail.com. We also provide a machine-readable event schedule JSON for client-side playback.
